# Chicken Fire
Chicken Fire is a simple website that presents a digital menu of the Chicken Fire restaurant. It has many features, like navigation bars and a contact section. It has many sections in the menu section, such as sandwiches, meals, pasta, offers, and appetizers.
#Why I made this website
Since I am a volunteer at Resala Charity in Egypt, I saw the restaurant owner pay money to print the menu, and the printing price of the menu is not small, so I created this website to lower the cost of printing the menu. Also, I mixed the technology and the restaurant's simple menu.
The second cause is when a customer gets a menu, he throws it after having his order or having it until it wears out, and he gets back to the restaurant a second time, and the scenario happens again. So this website is a permanent menu for each customer
The third is that after the menu is out of stock, the customers go to the cashier to look for the prices of the items, which makes the restaurant busy, and the orders are late because each customer looks at the menu, and then a new customer comes in to look at the menu. So this website helps the restaurant to prevent being busy and the customer to take their order earlier.

# How to use the website
1.	Scan the QR code to open the website or click the link 
2.	Navigate using the menu
3.	Explore each section to know the price of each product, the location, and the restaurant’s numbers
